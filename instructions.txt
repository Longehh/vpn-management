Prerequisites:
https://openvpn.net/client-connect-vpn-for-windows/ - OpenVPN Connect (VPN Client)


Important:
You will be given two files <name>.ovpn, and <name>-1.ovpn it’s important these files are NEVER shared to anyone but yourself.
The <name>.ovpn may not work for some people, if this happens proceed to use the <name>-1.ovpn.
You MUST NOT join any servers whilst connected to the VPN unless it’s the server you are working on.

Steps:
1. Go to the OpenVPN Link provided, and download your specific OS version.
2. After successfully downloading, run OpenVPN.
3. Download the profile(s) sent to you, starting the <name>.ovpn.
4. Drag/Import the profile(s) into the OpenVPN Client.
5. Turn on the profile.
6. Wait 10-20 seconds* and then attempt to load the relevant panel link.

Panel Link: ravenstone.azurax.net
